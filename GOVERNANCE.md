# Agency Governance & Roles

## 🏛 Mission Statement
The NSC Tech Hub operates as a system-dependent agency. We utilize a "Tree Architecture" to ensure that institutional knowledge is preserved and that cross-departmental collaboration (Art, Business, App Dev) is frictionless.

## 👥 Organizational Roles

### 1. Technical Program Manager (TPM)
* **Responsibility:** Acts as the "Trunk" of the agency. 
* **Mandate:** Translates stakeholder requirements into technical specifications, manages the GitHub Organization, and oversees the Vetting Loop.
* **Authority:** Final sign-off on technical architecture and system security.

### 2. Faculty Owners (Admins)
* **Responsibility:** Institutional oversight and curriculum alignment.
* **Mandate:** Ensure Tech Hub projects meet academic standards and provide high-value learning outcomes for students.

### 3. Department Liaisons (Lead Nodes)
* **Responsibility:** Managing internal department output (e.g., Art Lead, Business Lead).
* **Mandate:** Act as the single point of contact for their team. They ensure assets meet the "Design Specs" before submission to the TPM.

### 4. Production Teams (Students)
* **Responsibility:** Execution of tasks (Coding, Design, Marketing).
* **Mandate:** Work within "Team Repositories" and follow established documentation.
